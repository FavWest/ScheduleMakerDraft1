Schedule Maker

Welcome! This website is designed to help create a class schedule where you have a list of children who want certain days, or numbers of days, and a set number of spaces available each day.

To get started, enter a child's name and select their preferences. Say they want Monday and two other days, but NOT Friday: you would pick "Yes" for Monday, "No" for Friday, and leave the other days as "Ok".

If a child's family wants exactly 3 days, go to "Best" and select "3". If the family is more flexible, and prefers 3 days but would be willing to take a 2 day or 4 day schedule instead, you can enter "2" under "Minimum" and "4" under "Maximum".
